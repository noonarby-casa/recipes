## Instructions

1. Heat the butter or olive oil ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add the chopped sweet onion ({{< qty "1 small" >}}) and minced garlic ({{< qty "3 cloves" >}}).
3. Cook for {{< timer "4-5 minutes" >}} until the onion is translucent.
4. Add the sliced summer squash ({{< qty "4" >}}), fresh thyme ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), sea salt ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), and black pepper (to taste).
5. Cook for {{< timer "5-7 minutes" >}} until the squash is soft.
6. Add the water ({{< qty "4 cups" >}}) and bring the mixture to a boil.
7. Lower the heat to a simmer, cover, and cook for {{< timer "15-20 minutes" >}}.
8. Remove from heat. Carefully transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until smooth, adding the lemon zest (from {{< qty "1" >}} lemon) at the end of the blending process.
9. Return the soup to the pot over low heat. Stir in the heavy cream ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}) and fresh lemon juice ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) (plus more to taste).
10. Taste for seasoning and serve warm.
