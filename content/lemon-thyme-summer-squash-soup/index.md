## Instructions

1. Heat the butter or olive oil ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add the chopped sweet onion ({{< qty "1 small" >}}) and minced garlic ({{< qty "3 cloves" >}}).
3. Cook for {{< timer "4-5 minutes" >}} until the onion is translucent.
4. Add the sliced summer squash ({{< qty "4" >}}), dried thyme ({{< qty "1/2 teaspoon" >}}), sea salt ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), and black pepper (to taste).
5. Cook for {{< timer "5-7 minutes" >}} until the squash is soft.
6. Add the water ({{< qty "4 cups" >}}) and bring the mixture to a boil.
7. Lower the heat to a simmer, cover, and cook for {{< timer "15-20 minutes" >}}.
8. Remove from heat and carefully blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender (or in batches in a regular blender, being very careful with the hot liquid).
9. Stir in the lemon zest (from {{< qty "1" >}} lemon) and fresh lemon juice ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}), adding more juice if desired.
10. Taste for seasoning and serve warm.
