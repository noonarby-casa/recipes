## Instructions

### Stovetop Method

1. Heat the olive oil ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion ({{< qty "1" >}} yellow onion) and cook until fragrant, {{< timer "5 minutes" >}}.
2. Stir in the poblano peppers ({{< qty "1" >}} poblano pepper (or up to 2)), chicken ({{< qty "1 pound" >}}), chili powder ({{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}}), paprika ({{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}}), oregano ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Cook {{< timer "5 minutes" >}}, until you smell the seasonings.
3. Stir in the broth ({{< qty "6 cups" >}}) and salsa verde ({{< qty "2 cups" >}}). Season with salt and pepper. Partially cover and simmer over medium-low heat for {{< timer "15 minutes" >}}, until the chicken is cooked.
4. Shred the chicken using 2 forks, then stir back into the soup. Add the beans ({{< qty "1 can" >}}), lime juice ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}), and cilantro ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}). Cook {{< timer "10 minutes" >}} to warm through, or up to {{< timer "3 hours" >}} over the lowest heat possible. If needed, thin with additional broth.
5. Stir the rice ({{< qty "3 cups" >}}) into the soup, then ladle into bowls. Top, as desired, with yogurt, cheese, avocado, cilantro, and green onions. Grab a handful of tortilla chips, and serve crushed over the soup or alongside the soup for scooping. Zest lime over each bowl. Enjoy!

### Crockpot / Slow Cooker Method

1. In the bowl of your crockpot, combine the onion ({{< qty "1" >}} yellow onion), poblano peppers ({{< qty "1" >}} poblano pepper (or up to 2)), chicken ({{< qty "1 pound" >}}), chili powder ({{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}}), paprika ({{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}}), oregano ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Stir in the broth ({{< qty "6 cups" >}}) and salsa verde ({{< qty "2 cups" >}}).
2. Cover and cook on low for {{< timer "6-7 hours" >}} or high for {{< timer "4-5 hours" >}}.
3. Shred the chicken using two forks. Stir in the beans ({{< qty "1 can" >}}), lime juice ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}), and cilantro ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}).
4. Stir the rice ({{< qty "3 cups" >}}) into the soup, then ladle into bowls. Top, as desired, with yogurt, cheese, avocado, cilantro, and green onions. Grab a handful of tortilla chips, and serve crushed over the soup or alongside the soup for scooping. Zest lime over each bowl. Enjoy!
