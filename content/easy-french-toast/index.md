## Instructions

1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs ({{< qty "3 large" >}}), vanilla extract ({{< qty "3 teaspoons" >}}), and ground cinnamon ({{< qty "1 1/2 teaspoons" >}}). Stir in the milk ({{< qty "3/4 cup" >}}) until well combined.
2. Dip the brioche slices ({{< qty "12" >}}) into the egg mixture, turning them to ensure both sides are evenly coated.
3. Cook the brioche slices on a lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet over medium heat until cooked through and browned on both sides, about {{< timer "3-4 minutes" >}} per side.
