## Instructions

1. Add the Greek yogurt ({{< qty "1/3 cup" >}}), almond milk ({{< qty "2/3 cup" >}}), and maple syrup ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), if using, to a medium bowl and stir until well combined and creamy. Stir in the chia seeds ({{< qty "1/2 tablespoon" >}}) and rolled oats ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}). Cover the bowl and place in the fridge to chill for {{< timer "2-3 hours" >}} or overnight.
2. Once ready to assemble, spoon the overnight oats into a {{< qty "8 ounce" >}} mason jar or glass, leaving a small amount of room between the top surface of the oats and the rim of the jar for the topping.
3. Make the chocolate topping by microwaving the chocolate chips ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) and coconut oil ({{< qty "1/4 teaspoon" >}}) in a small bowl in {{< timer "30 seconds" >}} intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is melted.
4. Top the jar of overnight oats with the peanut butter ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), then with the melted chocolate mixture ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), spreading the chocolate to the rim of the jar so that it completely covers the peanut butter.
5. Place the jars in the freezer for {{< timer "10 minutes" >}} or until the chocolate hardens. If you’d like, sprinkle the tops with a little flaky sea salt. Use a spoon to crack open the chocolate shell and enjoy!
