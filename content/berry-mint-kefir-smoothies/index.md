## Instructions

1. Combine kefir ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), berries ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), orange juice ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), fresh mint ({{< qty "1-2 tablespoons" >}}), and honey ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}) in a blender.
2. Process until smooth. (The smoothies will keep in the refrigerator for up to 1 day or in the freezer for up to 3 months.)

## Tips

- Kefir is a drinkable yogurt found in the dairy section of most supermarkets.
