## Instructions

1. Thread {{< qty "1 package" >}} grape tomatoes, {{< qty "1 container" >}} mozzarella balls, and fresh basil onto skewers, arranging each skewer as: tomato, basil, mozzarella, basil, tomato.
2. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the finished skewers and serve.
