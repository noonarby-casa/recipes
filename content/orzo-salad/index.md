## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Prepare the orzo ({{< qty "1 1/2 cups" >}}) according to the package directions, or until slightly past al dente (about {{< timer "8 minutes" >}}). Drain the orzo and toss it with a little olive oil, so that it doesn’t stick together. Spread onto a baking sheet to cool.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the dressing ingredients: olive oil ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), red wine vinegar ({{< qty "4 tablespoons" >}}), lemon juice ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), minced garlic ({{< qty "1 clove" >}}), oregano ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), Dijon mustard ({{< qty "1/4 teaspoon" >}}), sea salt ({{< qty "1/2 teaspoon" >}}), and freshly ground black pepper.
3. In a large bowl, toss together the cooked orzo, cucumbers ({{< qty "2" >}} Persian), cherry tomatoes ({{< qty "2 cups" >}}), chickpeas ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), feta ({{< qty "4 ounces" >}}), red onion ({{< qty "1/3 cup" >}}), and olives ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}). Drizzle the dressing over the salad, add half the herbs ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), season with black pepper, and toss to coat. Garnish with the remaining herbs and serve.
