## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high.
2. While the water comes to a boil, prepare the dressing: In a large bowl, stir together the pickles ({{< qty "2/3 cup" >}}), scallions ({{< qty "4" >}}), mayonnaise ({{< qty "3/4 cup" >}}), milk ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), parsley ({{< qty "1/3 cup" >}}), dill ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), Dijon mustard ({{< qty "4 teaspoons" >}}), lemon zest ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), lemon juice ({{< qty "4 teaspoons" >}}) (from {{< qty "1 large" >}} lemon), and sugar ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}). Season with {{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}} salt and {{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}} pepper.
3. Cook the macaroni ({{< qty "16 ounces" >}}) in the boiling water until al dente, about {{< timer "6 minutes" >}}; drain well and let cool for a few minutes.
4. Once cooled, toss the macaroni with the dressing, and season to taste with additional salt and pepper. Serve immediately or refrigerate until chilled. Top with extra scallions, parsley, and dill to garnish just before serving.
