## Instructions

1. Rinse the chicken thighs ({{< qty "2 1/4 pounds" >}}), remove the bones, leaving the skin on, and pat them dry with paper towels. Set aside.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the lime juice ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}), lime zest ({{< qty "3 teaspoons" >}}), olive oil ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), chopped cilantro ({{< qty "4 tablespoons" >}}), chopped jalapeño ({{< qty "2" >}}), minced garlic ({{< qty "4 cloves" >}}), honey ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), salt ({{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}}), and chili powder ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}) until well combined.
3. Add the chicken to the marinade, tossing to coat evenly. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for {{< timer "2 hours" >}}.
4. Fire up the grill and brush a little oil on the surface. Add the chicken, placing some of the garlic, cilantro, and jalapeño from the marinade on top of the chicken. Grill until golden brown and charred on both sides, about {{< timer "10 minutes" >}} total, until cooked through. Serve immediately.
