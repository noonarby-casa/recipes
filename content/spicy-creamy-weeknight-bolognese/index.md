## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Meanwhile, heat a large Dutch oven or heavy pot over high until very hot. Add the beef ({{< qty "1 pound" >}}), and smush and spread in a single layer. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then cook, stirring, to break into tiny bits and cook through, {{< timer "4-5 minutes" >}}.
3. Push the meat to one side, and add the onion ({{< qty "1 large" >}}) and {{< qty "2" >}} carrots to the beef fat on the other side. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and cook, stirring occasionally with the beef, until the onion is translucent, about {{< timer "5 minutes" >}}.
4. Add the curry paste ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}} (or up to 3)), and stir until darkened in color, {{< timer "2-3 minutes" >}}. Pour in the tomato sauce ({{< qty "1 can" >}}), then fill the can a third of the way up with water to swish out the rest of the sauce into the pot. Stir, bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and simmer while the pasta cooks.
5. Cook the pasta ({{< qty "1 pound" >}}) in the boiling water until al dente. Save {{< qty "1/2 cup" >}} pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta and add to the simmering sauce.
6. Stir the cream ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}) into the sauced pasta, taste and season with salt and pepper. For a thinner sauce, stir in a bit of the pasta water. Serve hot.
