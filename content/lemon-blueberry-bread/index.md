## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the melted butter ({{< qty "1/3 cup" >}}), sugar ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), lemon juice ({{< qty "3 tablespoons" >}}), and eggs ({{< qty "2" >}}). Whisk smooth.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour ({{< qty "1 1/2 cups" >}}), baking powder ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), baking soda ({{< qty "1/4 teaspoon" >}}), and salt ({{< qty "1/2 teaspoon" >}}). Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture alternately with the sour cream ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}), stirring gently. Fold in lemon zest ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) and blueberries ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}). Do not over-mix.
3. Pour into a well-greased 8x4 inch loaf pan. Bake at 375°F for {{< timer "20 minutes" >}}. At the 20-minute mark, turn the oven down to 350°F. Bake for an additional {{< timer "30 to 40 minutes" >}}. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for {{< timer "10 minutes" >}}. Then remove to wire rack.
4. Whisk together the powdered sugar ({{< qty "3/4 cup" >}}) and lemon juice ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) to form a glaze. Drizzle glaze over the bread while still warm.
