## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix the oyster sauce ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}) and soy sauce ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}), and set aside.
2. In a very large skillet over medium heat, heat {{< qty "1 1/2 teaspoons" >}} of the canola oil. Add the eggs ({{< qty "2" >}}) and scramble just until set, about {{< timer "20 seconds" >}}. Pour the eggs into a small bowl and set aside.
3. Return the skillet to the burner, adjust the heat to high, add the remaining canola oil ({{< qty "2 1/2 tablespoons" >}}), and heat until it ripples.
4. Add the onion ({{< qty "1 medium" >}}) and carrots ({{< qty "3 medium" >}}), and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion starts to turn translucent, about {{< timer "2 minutes" >}}.
5. Add the peas ({{< qty "4 ounces" >}}) and cook, stirring, until they are barely tender-crisp, about {{< timer "1 1/2 minutes" >}}.
6. Add the garlic ({{< qty "3 cloves" >}}), toss to combine, and cook until fragrant, about {{< timer "30 seconds" >}}.
7. Add the rice ({{< qty "4 cups" >}}) and oyster-soy sauce mixture, stir to combine, and cook, stirring constantly, until the rice is heated through and evenly coated, about {{< timer "3 minutes" >}}.
8. Add the sesame oil ({{< qty "2 teaspoons" >}}), salt ({{< qty "1/2 teaspoon" >}}), pepper to taste, the scrambled eggs, and most of the scallions ({{< qty "3/4 cup" >}}), and toss to combine.
9. Transfer the fried rice to a heated serving bowl, sprinkle with the remaining scallions, and serve at once.
