## Instructions

### Jalapeño Cream Sauce

1. Place a pan on the stove over medium heat. Add the olive oil ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), roughly chopped red onion ({{< qty "1/4" >}}), roughly chopped garlic ({{< qty "2 cloves" >}}), cumin ({{< qty "1 1/2 teaspoons" >}}), and a pinch of salt and black pepper to taste. Sauté until the onion begins to soften, about {{< timer "5-7 minutes" >}}, stirring regularly.
2. Add the roughly chopped jalapeño ({{< qty "1" >}}) and vegan cream cheese ({{< qty "8 ounces" >}}) to the pan and sauté for an additional {{< timer "1-2 minutes" >}}, or until the cream cheese is softened and everything is combined.
3. Place the contents of the pan in a blender with the cilantro ({{< qty "1/2 bunch" >}}) and water ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}). Pulse until combined. Add additional water (up to {{< qty "1/2 cup" >}} total) if needed and taste to adjust seasoning. Place in an airtight container and keep in the fridge until ready to use.

### Enchilada Casserole

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Heat the olive oil ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}) in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the diced yellow onion ({{< qty "1/2" >}}), diced red bell pepper ({{< qty "1" >}}), diced green bell pepper ({{< qty "1" >}}), frozen corn ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), diced jalapeño ({{< qty "1" >}}), cumin ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), chili powder ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), and salt and black pepper to taste. Lower the heat to medium and cook, stirring frequently, until the veggies have begun to brown and are slightly softened, about {{< timer "5-7 minutes" >}}.
3. Remove the veggies from the heat, add half of the prepared Jalapeño Cream Sauce to the pan, and stir to combine.
4. Add the refried beans ({{< qty "1 can" >}}) to a bowl with {{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}} of water and stir to combine.
5. To assemble, spread {{< qty "1/2 cup" >}} of green enchilada sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer {{< qty "4" >}} corn tortillas on top of the enchilada sauce, followed by half of the refried beans, half of the black beans ({{< qty "1 can" >}}), and half of the veggies.
6. Repeat the layers, beginning with {{< qty "1/2 cup" >}} of green enchilada sauce, then {{< qty "4" >}} corn tortillas, the remaining refried beans, the remaining black beans, and the remaining veggies.
7. To finish it off, layer the final {{< qty "4" >}} corn tortillas on top, followed by the remaining {{< qty "1/2 cup" >}} of green enchilada sauce.
8. Place in the oven and cook for {{< timer "25 minutes" >}}, uncovered.
9. Increase the oven heat to broil and cook for an additional {{< timer "5 minutes" >}}, or until the top begins to brown. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for {{< timer "10-15 minutes" >}}.
10. Top the casserole with jalapeño slices, the remaining Jalapeño Cream Sauce, and cilantro. Slice and serve!
