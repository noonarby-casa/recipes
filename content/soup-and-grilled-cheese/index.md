## Instructions

1. Heat the {{< qty "1 quart" >}} of soup in a pot on medium-low.
2. Assemble the grilled cheese: assemble the bread (using the {{< qty "1" >}} loaf) and cheese (from the {{< qty "1" >}} package), and add the {{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}} of mayo to the outside of the slices.
3. Cook the grilled cheese on a griddle or flat pan over medium heat, about {{< timer "4-6 minutes" >}} per side, until the bread is golden and crispy.
4. Cut the grilled cheese in half, triangles, or strips, and serve with the warm soup.
