## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
2. In a food processor, pulse the shortbread cookies ({{< qty "9 ounces" >}}) until they reach the consistency of coarse sand.
3. Transfer the crumbs to a medium bowl, add the sugar ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}) and melted butter ({{< qty "4 tablespoons" >}}), and stir until combined.
4. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan or pie dish.
5. Bake for {{< timer "8-10 minutes" >}}, or until just golden and puffed.
6. Set aside to cool completely before adding the filling.
7. In a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, combine the egg yolks ({{< qty "6" >}}), sugar ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), and salt (1 pinch). Whip on high speed until the mixture is light in color, about {{< timer "3-4 minutes" >}}.
8. With the mixer running, slowly add the sweetened condensed milk ({{< qty "1 can" >}} - 14 ounces), followed by the lemon zest ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) and lemon juice ({{< qty "3/4 cup" >}}).
9. Pour the filling into the cooled crust.
10. Freeze for at least {{< timer "6 hours" >}} (or up to overnight).
11. When ready to serve, remove the pie from the freezer, un-mold it, slice into wedges, and serve with freshly whipped cream.
