## Instructions

1. **Toast Toppings**: Heat the vegetable oil ({{< qty "2 tablespoons" >}}) in a pan or skillet over medium heat (or under the broiler). Add the sliced almonds ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}) and sesame seeds ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), and cook until lightly toasted, about {{< timer "3-5 minutes" >}}. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
2. **Prep Salad**: Finely slice the green onions ({{< qty "8" >}}), thinly slice or chop the cabbage ({{< qty "1 head" >}}), and break up the ramen noodles ({{< qty "2" >}} packages) (you can pound the packages with a mallet while still closed).
3. **Combine Salad**: In a large salad bowl, combine the prepared green onions, cabbage, broken ramen noodles, cooked chicken ({{< qty "2 cups" >}}), and the cooled toasted almond and sesame seed mixture.
4. **Make Dressing**: In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the sugar ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), black pepper ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), salt ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), vegetable oil ({{< qty "1 cup" >}}), and rice vinegar ({{< qty "6 tablespoons" >}}) until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is well combined.
5. **Toss and Chill**: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to combine. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least {{< timer "2 hours" >}} (or up to several hours) before serving to let the flavors meld and the noodles soften slightly.
