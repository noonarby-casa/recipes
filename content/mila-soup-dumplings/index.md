## Instructions

1. In a large wok or pan, bring 2-3 inches of water to a rolling boil.
2. Place the Mila soup dumplings ({{< qty "1 package" >}}) in a lined steamer basket. Make sure to leave a 1 inch gap between dumplings.
3. Steam dumplings for {{< timer "11 minutes" >}} (until internal temp. reaches 165°F). Remove from heat.
4. Rest dumplings for {{< timer "1 minute" >}} with lid on. After 1 minute remove lid and rest for an additional {{< timer "1 minute" >}}. Serve with your favorite sauces!
