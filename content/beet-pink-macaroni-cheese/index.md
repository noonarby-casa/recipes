## Instructions

1. **Roast**: Preheat the oven to 425°F convection or 450°F standard. Wash and peel the beets ({{< qty "1 pound" >}}), then chop them into 1-inch cubes. Cut the top off a head of garlic ({{< qty "1" >}} head). Place the beets and garlic on foil, drizzle the garlic with a little olive oil, and wrap tightly. Roast for {{< timer "40-45 minutes" >}} until the beets are tender.
2. **Prep & Water**: Set a large pot of salted water to boil. While it heats, zest the lemon ({{< qty "1" >}}) and halve it. Grate the parmesan ({{< qty "3 ounces" >}}).
3. **Blend**: Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves out of their skins into a blender. Add the roasted beets, lemon zest, grated parmesan, and a few tablespoons of water. Blend until smooth.
4. **Cook Pasta**: Once the pot of water is boiling, cook the rigatoni pasta ({{< qty "16 ounces" >}}) until al dente. Reserve {{< qty "1 cup" >}} of pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta.
5. **Assemble**: Return the hot pasta to the pot. Pour in the olive oil ({{< qty "1/4 cup" >}}), dried basil ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), lemon juice to taste, and the blended beet sauce. Mix until smooth, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy coating. Season with salt to taste and serve warm.
