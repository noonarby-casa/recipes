## Instructions

1. If serving with rice, start cooking the basmati rice.
2. Mince the onion ({{< qty "1" >}} yellow onion) and garlic ({{< qty "1 large" >}} garlic clove). Peel and mince or grate the ginger ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}). Drain and rinse the chickpeas ({{< qty "2 cans" >}}).
3. In a large skillet, heat the oil ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}) over medium-high heat. Add the onion and saute for {{< timer "5 minutes" >}}. Add the garlic, ginger, and spinach ({{< qty "5 ounces" >}}) and saute for {{< timer "2 minutes" >}} until the spinach is fully wilted.
4. Carefully pour in the tomatoes ({{< qty "1 can" >}}), then add the curry powder ({{< qty "1 tablespoon" >}}), cumin ({{< qty "1 teaspoon" >}}), coriander ({{< qty "1/2 teaspoon" >}}), salt ({{< qty "3/4 teaspoon" >}}), and chickpeas. Cook for {{< timer "5 minutes" >}} until bubbly.
5. Stir in the coconut milk ({{< qty "1/2 cup" >}}) until fully combined, then remove from the heat. Serve warm with basmati rice.
