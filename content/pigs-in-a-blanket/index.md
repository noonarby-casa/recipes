## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
2. Using the {{< qty "1 can" >}} of Pillsbury crescent rolls, cut each triangle into thirds. Place one cocktail weenie from the {{< qty "1 package" >}} onto each piece of dough, roll it up, and arrange the bites on an ungreased baking sheet.
3. Bake for {{< timer "10-13 minutes" >}} until the dough is golden brown.
4. Remove from the oven and serve with ketchup.
